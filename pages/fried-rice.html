Haiyaa, Egg Fried Rice

Description

Haiyaa, egg fried rice also need tutorial? It's so simple, you just need egg, fried and rice. Come, let uncle teach you, in 5 minutes you will be better than the Jamie Oliver.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp. Peanut Oil
  • 5 Garlic Cloves, smashed
  • 1 Shallot, sliced from the middle
  • 1 Egg +1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 Spring Onion, sliced
  • 1 Red Chili, sliced or chopped
  • 1 bowl of day old rice (Freshly cooked rice also works, but not as great)
  • About 2 tbsp. Soy Sauce
  • About 2 tbsp. Asian Sesame Oil
  • Â½ tbsp. MSG

Steps

  1. Whisk the whole egg and egg yolk in a small bowl and set aside
  2. Coat a wok pan (or regular frying pan if you dont have one) with peanut oil over medium heat until smoking
  3. Pour out the excess oil and add garlic and shallots to fry for 3 minutes
  4. Add the beaten egg into the pan and stir until almost solid
  5. Add the rice and mix with everything in the wok
  6. Add in the soy sauce and sesame oil
  7. Add the MSG, spring onion and chili to the wok and fry for 1 minute
  8. Plate your delicious asian egg fried rice and enjoy!
